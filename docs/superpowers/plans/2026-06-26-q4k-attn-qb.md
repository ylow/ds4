# Q4_K attention `q_b` extension — Implementation Plan

> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`).

**Goal:** Extend the Q4_K attention-weight recipe to also requantize `attn_q_b` (the 1024×32768 query up-projection, ~33.5M params/layer — as large as a whole output tensor and ~85% of the q/kv byte budget), measuring the additional nll / decode-tps effect. Follow-on to `2026-06-26-q4k-attn-output.md`.

**Architecture:** **No new CUDA kernels.** `attn_q_b`'s matmul already runs non-fused on both paths: decode = plain `ds4_gpu_matmul_q8_0_tensor` then a *separate* rms+rope; prefill has a non-fused `else` branch (plain matmul then separate `ds4_gpu_head_rms_norm_tensor` + `ds4_gpu_rope_tail_tensor`). So we (1) regenerate the GGUF with `q_b` also Q4_K, (2) dispatch `q_b`'s matmul to the existing `ds4_gpu_matmul_q4k_tensor`, forcing the non-fused prefill path for Q4_K, (3) relax 2 validation sites. The rms/rope steps are weight-format-agnostic and unchanged.

**Tech Stack:** C11 (`ds4.c`), the existing Q4_K kernels from the prior plan, `gguf-tools/deepseek4-quantize` (no change). Build: `make cuda-spark`.

- **Dispatch by loaded type** (`attn_q_b->type == DS4_TENSOR_Q4_K`, =12). Original GGUFs stay valid; rollback = symlink.

## Results (q_b increment)

**GGUF:** `DeepSeek-V4-Flash-IQ2XXS-w2Q2K-AProjQ8-AOutQbQ4K-SExpQ8-OutQ8-chat-v2-imatrix.gguf` — **84.556 GB** (84,555,850,336 bytes)

### Perplexity (`-n 256 -c 4096`)

| Config | nll | ppl | Δnll vs Q8 | Δnll vs output-only Q4_K |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Q8 baseline | 319.009406860 | 3.4769 | — | — |
| output-only Q4_K | 316.384665147 | 3.4414 | −2.625 (−0.823%) | — |

|---|---|---|
| Q8 baseline | 15.11 | 14.82 |

### Summary

Adding `attn_q_b` to the Q4_K recipe saves ~0.72 GB vs the output-only Q4_K GGUF (85.277 → 84.556 GB) and increases decode throughput by +0.63 t/s at ctx 4096 and +0.64 t/s at ctx 16384 (a ~4% further gain on top of the output-only improvement). However, nll regresses +4.652 (+1.458%) relative to the Q8 baseline and +7.276 (+2.300%) relative to the output-only Q4_K, making the output+q_b GGUF the lowest-quality of the three measured configurations; the q_b tensor (1024×32768, the query up-projection) is quality-sensitive at Q4_K. The accept/reject decision is the user's.
